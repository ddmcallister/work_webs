Some of the Types of Services Offered by the MAP Staff Are:

  • Personal and Family Counseling Sessions
  • Information and Referrals
  • Vocational Services/Assistance
  • Pre-Apprentice and Apprentice Presentations
  • Resource Manuals and Brochures
  • Site Visits and Worksite Specific Interventions
  • Regular Newsletter Updates

Is the Counseling Really Confidential?

YES! Everything discussed in counseling sessions is completely confidential except for situations that require preventing serious harm (to self or others) or stopping the abuse of a child or elderly person.

What Are the Benefits for Me?

It can be difficult to balance the demands of a training program, career, and personal obligations. The MAP counselors are here to assist you for free in balancing those demands by providing one-on-one counseling, seminars, trainings, referral to outside resources, and much, much more.

All you have to do is make the call to the MAP.
