Referral Hall Overview for Contractors

Overview

Contractors who are signatory to the new CBA are required to use the Voluntary Referral Hall for 1 out of every 4 new hires—but the VRH is also available for any other workforce needs you may have.

If a contractor needs to hire a Local 28 journeyperson for fan maintenance work, the contractor must use the VRH.

Any contractor who is not a signatory to Local 28's CBA (including those who are signatories to the International Agreement and out-of-town contractors) who need to hire Local 28 members must use the VRH. Such contractors should contact the appropriate Business Agent.

Please note: jobs at the B rate, specialty work, and apprentice referrals are not placed through the Referral Hall.

How does it work?

Out of work L28 mechanics telephone the VRH and place themselves on the list. During this call mechanics can indicate their special skills (certified welder, roofer), if any.

During referral hours (6pm – 8pm, Monday – Friday), the Operator calls any open jobs. The computer selects the highest ranking available journeyperson with the necessary skills and contacts that member by telephone.

If the journeyperson does not answer, or refuses the job, the next highest ranking journeyperson is contacted. This process continues until the referral is accepted.

Making a Request

Contractors can place job requests several ways. See the next page for tips on making effective requests!

By fax: Complete the Contractor Workforce Request Form. Fax it to 212-732-5440.

By phone: Call and speak to an Operator at 212-732-5979. Operators are available Monday - Friday, 9 am to 5 pm.

Via the internet: Complete the online Contractor Workforce Request Form and click the submit button.
